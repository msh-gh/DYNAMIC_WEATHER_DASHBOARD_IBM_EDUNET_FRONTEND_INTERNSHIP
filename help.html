Need Help? Here you go!

I. Search Bar

Acceptable Keywords:

  • A US Zip Code. Example: 10012 (For New York, NY)
  • A Canadian Zip Code. Example: V0J2J1 (For New Hazelton, BC)
  • A Place. Example: Omaha, NE, or Paris, FR
  • Latitude and Longitude. Example: 38.44,-83.19
  • User's IP. Example: :auto
  • USA NOAA Public Alert Zone. Example: GAZ033, or zone:GAZ033 (For North Fulton County, GA)
  • Canadian Location Code. Example: 029100 (For Churchill Falls, NL). Go to Here to find your location code.
  • A 4 Digit Airport Code. Example: KJFK, or UUEE (For John F. Kennedy Airport in New York, NY, and Moscow Sheremetyevo Alexander S. Pushkin International Airport in Moscow, RU)

II. Conditions

Conditions are updated about every minute, and the following weather details are provided:

  • Current temperature in fahrenheit and celcius
  • The Feels Like Temperature
  • Relative Humidity
  • Dew Point
  • UV Index, UV stands for Ultra-violet, and a note if the UV is over 5.
  • Wind in miles per hour and kilometers per hour
  • Visibility
  • Barometric Pressure, for aviation
  • Rain Rate, indicates how fast the rain is falling
  • Snow Depth, Indicating how much snow is on the ground
  • Solar Radiation in watts per meter squared.

You will also see a radar with satalite imagery, lightning strikes, global radar, and city names.

III. Forecasts

The Forecasts Provide up to 7 days ahead with the cloud cover, high temperature, and low temperature.

IV. Closest Lightning Stike

The Following is Provided:

  • A Caption Stating the closest lightning strike.
  • A map showing where that lightning struck.

You may also see a note for if nothing is displayed.

V. Hourly Forecast

The Following is Provided:

  • Up to 3 hours in depth of what the weather will be like, and the temperature.
  • A button switching over to metric units.

VI. Errors

  • Error Type 1: Maximum API Calls Daily:

This means that the amount of searches made daily hit the max, so you may have to wait until the next day.

  • Error Type 2: Incorrect Search:

If this occurs, please make sure you typed in your city correctly.

  • Error Type 3: Content not displaying:

This means that some of the content could not be displayed. Open a new tab, and copy & paste the web page you were on before.
